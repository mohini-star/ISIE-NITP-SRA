ABOUT US

ISIE NITP SRA stands for IMPERIAL SOCIETY OF INNOVATIVE ENGINEERS
NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY PATNA STUDENT RESEARCH ASSOCIATION

This club aims at creating value through innovation, quality, creativity, partnerships, openness, and learning. It is the only club of NITP which is working on renewable sources of energy. We set out with an obsession for concept development, one that was unheard in the corridors of green energy development.
